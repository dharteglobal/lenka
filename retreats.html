What is Panchakarma?

Panchakarma is an ancient Ayurvedic cleansing protocol designed to deeply detoxify and rejuvenate your entire system. Combined with meditation, yoga, and Lenka's therapeutic support, this retreat creates the conditions for profound healing and renewal. Many participants describe it as the most restorative experience of their lives.
