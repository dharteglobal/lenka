Art & Drawing

Art as meditation, healing, and creative expression

Classes for all levels · Online & in Prague

Creative Expression Through Drawing

Lenka is an accomplished artist and drawing instructor. Beyond her therapeutic practice, she teaches fine art and drawing through Learn Drawing, bringing her belief that "art is a form of meditation when we can fully switch off the mind and concentrate on the creative process."

Her artistic background — trained at the Václav Hollar School of Fine Arts in Prague, with study in puppetry and a decade of professional graphic design — deeply informs her therapeutic approach. She uses creative expression, visualisation, and artistic techniques as powerful tools for healing and nervous system integration.

"I believe everyone can draw. Whether you are just starting out, returning after years away, or already have some technique — the point is the quality of presence you bring to the page." — Lenka Lorien
