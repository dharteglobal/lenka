Healing Through Presence

Lenka Lorien is a somatic therapist and healing practitioner based in Prague, with over 15 years of clinical experience. She specializes in trauma recovery, nervous system regulation, and body-centered healing using Somatic Experiencing® and biodynamic craniosacral therapy.

Three Foundations of Every Session

"Healing rests on touch, a sense of safety, and the deep experience of being truly heard."

Healing Modalities

Drama & Voice Therapy
