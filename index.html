Meet Lenka Lorien

Artist turned somatic therapist. With over 15 years of clinical experience, Lenka facilitates nervous system healing through intentional touch, presence, and deep listening. Trained in Somatic Experiencing®, biodynamic craniosacral therapy, Thai massage, and traditional Ayurvedic healing.

Her unique blend of artistic sensitivity and rigorous therapeutic training creates a rare capacity to meet clients with wholeness and compassion.
