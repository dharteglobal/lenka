How I Work

Somatic Experiencing® (developed by Dr. Peter Levine) helps the body complete the survival responses that got stuck during trauma — fight, flight, or freeze. I guide you to gently discharge that activation from your nervous system, so your body can finally feel safe again.

Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy works with the subtle rhythms of your cerebrospinal fluid and the body's own healing intelligence. It's so gentle it feels like a feather's touch — yet incredibly effective for releasing deep tension.

Hypnotherapy and body awareness practices deepen the work, helping you access your own inner resources and wisdom.

Every session is held by trust, safety, and the quiet discipline of truly listening.

About Lenka

I began my professional life as an artist — studying fine art and graphic design — but a turn toward the body transformed my path entirely. Since 2009, I've been working with the nervous system, specializing in Somatic Experiencing and Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy.

From May to October, I see clients in person in Prague. From November to April, I lead and organize Ayurvedic wellness retreats in India, creating spaces for deep rest and regeneration. I also facilitate healing workshops internationally.

My work is especially attuned to those navigating long-term or complex healing — encouraging personal responsibility while offering steady and compassionate guidance.
