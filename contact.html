Frequently Asked Questions

Is it OK if I've never done this before?

Absolutely. There's no experience needed. We start exactly where you are, and I'll guide you through everything. Many of my clients had never heard of somatic work before their first session.

How often should I come?

That depends on what you're working with. Some people come weekly for a period, others monthly. We'll talk about what makes sense for your situation and goals. There's no "should" — only what feels right for you.

What if I'm not in Prague?

All sessions are available online. Somatic work translates beautifully to video sessions — I can guide you through the work from anywhere, and you can remain in your own safe space at home.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes — anything you can move and breathe in. For craniosacral work, you stay fully clothed; there is no massage oil or disrobing involved.

Do you work with children?

Yes, I have specialist training in working with newborns, infants, children, and teenagers. Sessions are adapted to their age, developmental stage, and individual needs.
