Frequently Asked Questions

Is it OK if I've never done this before?

Absolutely. There's no experience needed. We start exactly where you are, and I'll guide you through everything. Many of my clients had never heard of somatic work before their first session.

How often should I come?

That depends on what you're working with. Some people come weekly for a period of time, others monthly. We'll talk about what makes sense for your situation and goals. There's no "should" — only what feels right for you.

What if I'm not in Prague?

All sessions are available online. Somatic work translates beautifully to video sessions — I can guide you through the work from anywhere, and you can remain in your own safe space at home.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes — anything you can move and breathe in. For craniosacral work, you'll stay fully clothed; there's no massage oil or disrobing.

Do you work with children?

Yes, I have special training in working with children and teenagers. Sessions are adapted to their age, developmental stage, and needs.
