About Lenka

From Artist to Healer

The Journey

Artist first. Lenka began in fine art and graphic design, studying and practising for several years. Creativity remains woven through all her work today — expressed in imagery, symbolism and a finely tuned sensitivity.

Then healer. In 2009 she was drawn to the body. She moved to England, working with children and the elderly, as a body therapist at festivals, and studying holistic medicine. There she completed a two-year Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy certification in London (2009–2011), followed by three years in Dr. Peter Levine's Somatic Experiencing method (2014–2017).

Now both. With postgraduate training in working with mothers, babies and birth trauma — plus Thai massage, Kundalini yoga, TRE and Ayurveda — every session brings artistic sensitivity, clinical depth and unwavering presence.

India shaped Lenka deeply. Repeated journeys opened her to Ayurveda, meditation and traditional teachings, and meeting the Dalai Lama and Tibetan Buddhist practice cultivated the compassion, peace and mindful presence she now brings to every session.
