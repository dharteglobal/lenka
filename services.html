Individual Sessions

Each session draws from the modalities your nervous system needs in that moment. No two sessions are identical.

🧠 Somatic Experiencing® Therapy

Evidence-based trauma therapy developed by Dr. Peter Levine. Works directly with the nervous system to help your body complete unfinished survival responses and find safety again.

  • Trauma recovery and PTSD support
  • Nervous system regulation and freeze responses
  • Chronic pain and somatic symptoms
  • Anxiety, panic, and dysregulation
  • Available in-person (Prague) or online

💆 Biodynamic Craniosacral Therapy

Light-touch therapy working with cerebrospinal fluid rhythms and the body's innate healing intelligence. Effective for releasing deep tension and supporting whole-system healing.

  • Adult craniosacral sessions
  • Specialised work with newborns and infants
  • Birth trauma and post-natal support
  • Mothers, children, and families
  • In-person Prague · May–October

🙌 Thai Therapeutic Massage

Traditional Thai massage trained in Chiang Mai — combining acupressure, assisted stretching, and energy work. Releases held tension and promotes deep alignment.

  • Full-body Thai massage (60 or 90 min)
  • Focused sessions on specific areas
  • Energy balancing and alignment
  • In-person Prague · Summer and autumn

🎭 Drama Therapy & Voice Work

Expressive modalities accessing emotional truth through movement, voice, and creative play. Powerful for grief, shame, and inner child healing.

  • Voice work and vocal release
  • Drama therapy and creative expression
  • Movement and somatic play
  • Available in-person or online

👶 Family & Infant Support

Specialised work with pregnant women, new mothers, newborns, infants, children, and teens. Supporting healthy nervous system development at every life stage.

  • Prenatal and post-natal support
  • Infant craniosacral and somatic work
  • Birth trauma recovery
  • Bonding and attachment support
  • In-person Prague
