🌊 Craniosacral Therapy

Duration: 60 min | Format: In Prague (May–October only)

Biodynamic craniosacral therapy uses light touch to work with the rhythm of your cerebrospinal fluid, gently unwinding deep tension held in the nervous system. It feels like a feather's touch, yet is profoundly healing.

Who it's for: Anyone with tension, insomnia, headaches, neck/shoulder pain, or after injury. Especially powerful for chronic stress and nervous system dysregulation.

👶 Children & Family Sessions

Duration: 30–60 min | Format: Online or in Prague
